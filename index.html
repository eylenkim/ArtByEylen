About Me

Hello, my name is Eylen! I graduated from Western Washington University with a bachelors in Management Information Systems. Post-graduation, I decided to stay in Bellingham, WA for it's beautiful outdoors, cool art scene, and rad music culture.

I create art by: making music with my electric guitar, printmaking, acrylic, molding clay earrings, drawing, and taking photos with disposable/35mm cameras. Who knows what my next artistic endeavor is?

At WWU, I enjoyed coding & tech. I've picked up: web development skills (HTML, CSS), database management (SQL), and object-oriented programming languages (VB.NET, PHP, JavaScript).

I've always felt conflicted about separating tech and art. After my graduation, I realized that I couldn't work the rest of my life without incorporating art & design into technology. So here I am, world! This is my next gig: self-learning UX/UI design. Creating this website is a start. I am very thrilled to learn everyday!
