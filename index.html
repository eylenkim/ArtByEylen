About Me

Hello, my name is Eylen! I graduated from WWU with a bachelors in Management Information Systems. Post-graduation, I decided to stay in Bellingham, WA for it's beautiful outdoors, eccentric art scene, and rad music culture.

I've always felt conflicted about separating tech and art. I realized I couldn't work the rest of my life without design in the picture. So, here is the bread to my butter! I am self-learning UX/UI design concepts while coding my art portfolio website.

My art takes on a few forms: printmaking, acrylic paintings, molded clay earrings, lo-fi rock, and disposable/35mm photos.

At WWU, I really enjoyed coding and picked up: web development skills (HTML, CSS), database management (SQL), and object-oriented programming languages (VB.NET, PHP, JavaScript).
